About Us

“ইস্কুলঘর”—শৈশবের হাজারো স্নৃতিময় এক উপাখ্যান । এটা নিছক কোন শব্দ নয়। এর সাথে জড়িয়ে আছে রাঙ্গিয়ে যাওয়া স্নৃতি। মায়ের বকুনি, বই হাতে দলবল মিলে ছুটে চলা, কানামাছি, প্রথম স্যার, প্রথম ম্যাডাম, দেয়াল টপকে স্কুল পালানোর রহস্যময় গল্প—এমনি সব স্নৃতিময়তায় “ইস্কুলঘর” ভেসে ওঠে চোখে । শৈশবের সেই আনন্দকে সঙ্গী করেই আমাদের এই নতুন “ইস্কুলঘর” এর যাত্রা । এই “ইস্কুলঘর” এর কোন দেয়াল নেই, গৎবাঁধা পরীক্ষা নেই, কেবল আছে জানার সীমাহীন আগ্রহ, ভয়হীন প্রশ্ন ও তার সমাধানে গবেষণার আনন্দময় জগতে সকাল-সন্ধ্যা খেলা করা । সেই লক্ষ্যকে সামনে রেখেই কয়েকজন কর্মোদ্যমী গবেষণা পাগল তরুণদের নিয়ে “ইস্কুলঘর” এর যাত্রা । যার মূল উদ্দেশ্য গবেষণায় আগ্রহী শিক্ষার্থীদের গবেষণা ও উচ্চশিক্ষা বিষয়ে বিভিন্ন অনলাইন কোর্স ও সেমিনারের মাধ্যমে দক্ষ করে তোলা। এই বাস্তবসম্বত গবেষণার প্রশিক্ষণে যুক্ত হবেন দেশ ও দেশের বাইরে বিভিন্ন বিশ্ববিদ্যালয়ে গবেষণারত বাংলাদেশি শিক্ষক ও শিক্ষার্থী, যারা জানেন নতুন প্রজন্মের প্রাণের দাবি ।

Our Services

Online courses and training

Ishkulghar offers a wide range of online courses and training, ranging from fundamental to applied sciences. Our online courses provide an affordable and flexible option for you to gain new skills on emerging topics. Ishkulghar strives to improve the technical knowledge and expertise of undergraduate, postgraduate, MPhil, and Ph.D. students, professionals, and faculty engaged in research or practice. There is no prior experience with research necessary. Anyone with an interest and some basic knowledge is welcome to attend.

Career Skills and Certification

The career skills and certification program at Ishkulghar are designed to help you develop skills that may be useful on the job or to strengthen your resume and knowledge. Our team is comprised of a diverse and experienced number of professionals, researchers, and subject-matter experts that work together to provide relevant suggestions to assist steer your professional journey. If you're searching for employment, this program will help you gain industry-recognized qualifications and open doors to new opportunities. Our team of program advisors will be with you every step of the way, assisting you to achieve your goals and improve your capabilities.

Higher Studies Consultancy

Ishkulghar offers higher education consultancy to students seeking admission to the world's finest universities. We are a student-focused organization dedicated to offering fair and accurate support and counseling to students seeking admission to educational institutions/universities in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Japan, South Korea, Germany, Singapore, and other countries. Our experience knows the challenges you may face, such as exam preparation and scholarships. Our counselors are dedicated to assisting students by discussing numerous available alternatives and choices of various nations, colleges, and courses based on the student's information. This would assist us in determining the best course for a student and then providing him/her with possibilities for universities/colleges overseas.

Publication Aid

Iskulghar assists students, researchers, academics, and companies with the research process, which includes topic development, experiment design, literature review, methodological specifications, data analysis, interpretation, and manuscript editing. To mention a few, our team included specialists in biology, commerce, engineering, education, information technology, electronics & communications, computer science, chemistry, physics, and healthcare. We also help academic researchers who are writing or designing a study with the goal of publishing the results in reputed, indexed (SCI/SCOPUS/ISI/PUBMED), and peer-reviewed journals.

Scientific Session

Iskulghar welcomes top researchers in the life and applied sciences and organizes scientific presentations highlighting current research, successes, early career development prospects, and additional networking and mentorship opportunities. These sessions present participants with cutting-edge research, laboratory best practices, and collaborative solutions to research obstacles. Concise and focused talks on a variety of subjects are expected to interest guests while also offering opportunities for networking.
